Lift is a modern, high-performance fitness tracker designed for lifters who want a clean, distraction-free interface to manage their workouts and track their strength progress.

<b>Key Features:</b>

• <b>Workout Management:</b> Create custom workout routines, reorder exercises with drag-and-drop, and set target sets
• <b>Exercise Pool:</b> A searchable database of exercises with support for custom additions
• <b>Intelligent Tracking:</b> Smart placeholders automatically show your weight/reps from your last session for that specific workout. Focus on your lift without toggling checkboxes
• <b>Data Visualization:</b>
  - <i>Repetition History:</i> Scrollable timeline charts showing total volume progress
  - <i>Weight Tracker:</i> Monitor body weight with a dedicated chart and date/time-specific logs
  - <i>Interactive Timelines:</i> Swipe through history with range toggles (1W, 1M, 3M, All)

• <b>Modern UI:</b> A sleek, dark-themed interface built using Material 3 and an indigo-slate color palette
• <b>100% Offline:</b> Works completely offline - no internet connection required for any features
• <b>Local-First:</b> Fast performance and offline support using Hive NoSQL storage

<b>Privacy & Offline-First:</b>

Lift is designed with your privacy as the top priority. The app works 100% offline, requiring no internet connection. Your data never leaves your device - no cloud sync, no account creation, and no data transmission.

<b>No Data Collection:</b> We don't collect, share, or sell any of your data. No analytics services are used, and you can use the app immediately without creating an account or providing personal information.

Your data stays private. Since everything is stored locally, only you have access to your workout information.
