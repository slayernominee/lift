# Privacy Statement

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Overview

At Lift, we take your privacy seriously. This privacy statement explains how we handle your data when you use our fitness tracking application. Our commitment to you is simple: **your data stays on your device.**

## Data Collection and Storage

### What We Collect

Lift collects the following data to provide you with a complete fitness tracking experience:

- **Workout Data:** Exercise names, sets, repetitions, weights, and workout routines
- **Progress Tracking:** Historical workout records and performance metrics
- **Body Measurements:** Weight logs with timestamps (if you choose to track them)
- **User Preferences:** App settings, theme preferences, and workout configurations

### How We Store Your Data

- **Local Storage Only:** All your data is stored locally on your device using Hive NoSQL database
- **No Cloud Sync:** Lift does not sync, upload, or transmit any of your data to external servers
- **No Account Required:** You don't need to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app
- **Offline-First:** The app works entirely offline and does not require an internet connection for any of its features

## Data Sharing

### We Do NOT Share Your Data

- **No Third-Party Sharing:** We do not share, sell, rent, or lease your personal data to any third parties
- **No Analytics:** We do not use any analytics services that collect your usage data
- **No Tracking:** We do not track your behavior or collect telemetry data
- **No Advertising:** We do not serve ads or share data with advertising networks

## Your Rights and Control

### Data Ownership

- **You Own Your Data:** All data stored in the app belongs to you
- **Deletion:** You can delete your data at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app

### Data Security

- **Device Security:** Your data is protected by your device's security features (lock screen, encryption, etc.)
- **No Network Transmission:** Since data never leaves your device, there's no risk of data interception during transmission

## Children's Privacy

Lift is a general audience application and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we don't collect any personal information or create user accounts, this is not applicable to our service.

## Changes to This Privacy Statement

We may update this privacy statement from time to time. When we do, we will:

- Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document
- Make the new version available within the app and on our project repository

## Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions about this privacy statement or our privacy practices, please contact us through our GitHub repository:

- **GitHub Repository:** https://github.com/slayernominee/lift
- **Issues:** https://github.com/slayernominee/lift/issues

## Summary

**In short:** Your workout data, personal information, and app usage never leave your device. We don't collect analytics, don't serve ads, don't require accounts, and don't share your data with anyone. Your privacy is our priority.
